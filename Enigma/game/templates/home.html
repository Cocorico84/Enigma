The Crown wishes you good fortune in your quest detective

Find the murderers and punish them!

You are a detective dived into a sordid investigation through the beginning of the 20th century in a fictive town called Scoundrels-City ruled by The Crown. Your purpose is to catch the wretched who murdered people by greed, lust, hate or just for the pleasure of it!

For this city is full of drunken rats who plunder at any moment or sell their own mother for a coin. The murderers will be even more dangerous. We can't no longer tolerate their insolence toward The Crown, a team of detectives followed by heartless mercenaries have been mandated to end this sins!

Tutorial

Choose your cover!

Don't be caught during your investigation, the cover can help you to catch the murderers.
Create a cover inside the detective database with the api to enter the game.

Proceed with caution!

The filthy wretched won't hesitate a second to murder you if you get too close!

Don't play by the rules!

If you have been seen and the guilty escape you can murder them! The Crown is not very careful about ethics.
Well it's just criminals after all!
You have two databases at your disposal one for the caught and another for the killed.
The Crown would prefer if you avoid to kill too many people...this shouldn't be known.

Teamplay is everything...or not!

You might not be the only one who wants to want a reward from The Crown.
Don't be fooled! Do not arrest another undercover detective.

Use your budget...and control it!

Bribe the vermin if it can allow you to catch a better prize!
Gain information by corruption feeds the clues database.

Catch some morons!

Every guilty target you catch brings you money from The Crown.
Offenders prizes earn you lesser than the criminals.
Remember that the main quest is to punish the 5 murderers.

Learn some skills!

Some scums are strangely cleverer than others.
They won't be caught without the correct skill...at least if you want to catch them.

Hire mercenaries!

Some muscles won't be too much to catch these scoundrels.
Mercenaries from the kindgom are useful to get rid of a criminal...but really greedy and expensive.
When The Crown says "DEAD OR ALIVE" they say "NOT ALIVE"!

Use the Circle's Archives!

Your chiefs are at the head of a unique organization at the service of The Crown called the Circle.
It's perfectly GDPR and respectful of basic human rights...
Use the surveillance files to find the criminals or everyone else...honestly who cares?
